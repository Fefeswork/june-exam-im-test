GAME PROTOTYPES:

These are game prototypes I’ve worked on using the UNITY game engine during my time studying at WITS university.
During my free time I like to make changes and improve on prototypes that I received good results on.

For this visual novel I was focused on the narrative part of the game. The prompt given was ‘support’. Using this prompt, I explored the idea of the struggle of building courage to ask for help when you are going through a rough time. In the visual novel you play as a character who has just endured a season ending injury and now has to take on the challenge of asking for support/help(which is something athletes don’t often do due to having a winning and independent mindset they tend to have- I’ve referenced my own journey as an athlete myself) during her road to full recovery. I try to show this through the choices I present the player with.

This 2D platformer was a task I’ve enjoyed as I haven’t played many 2D platformer games before conquering this task. In this prototype I explore level design and what makes a level good. I analysed levels of games I’ve played and considered what made the level enjoyable. I incorporated level changes and made use of increasing difficulty after the player made it past a certain checkpoint in the level. The goal of game was to save the cherry king from the evil cupcake vigilante.
